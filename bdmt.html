Bun Dau Mam Tom

Bun dau mam tom is a popular Vietnamese dish known for its unique combination of flavors and textures. People often enjoy bun dau mam tom with fresh herbs and vegetables, such as lettuce, mint and cilantro, and slices of cucumber. The idea is to balance the strong, and pungent flavors of the shrimp paste with the freshness and crispness of the herbs and vegetables.

Ingredients

For Bun Dau:

  • 1 package of vermicelli noodles (rice noodles)
  • 1 block of firm tofu, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 pound pork belly or pork shoulder, thinly sliced oil for frying
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Fresh herbs (mint, cilantro, perilla leaves)
  • Cucumber, julienned
  • Green onions, chopped
  • Fried shallots (optional)

For Mam Tom (Fermented Shrimp Paste Sauce)

  • 1/4 cup fermented shrimp paste (mam tom)
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • Thai chili (optional), finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup water

Preparation

1

Prepare the Vermicelli Noodles

  1. Cook the vermicelli noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

2

Make the Mam Tom Sauce

  1. In a small bowl, mix the fermented shrimp paste (mam tom) with sugar, lime juice, minced garlic, Thai chili (if using), and water. Stir until well combined. Adjust the sweetness or spiciness to your taste. Set the sauce aside.

3

Prepare the Toppings:

  1. Slice the tofu into cubes and fry them in oil until golden brown. Drain on paper towels.
  2. Slice the pork belly or pork shoulder thinly.
  3. Wash and prepare the lettuce leaves, fresh herbs, julienned cucumber, chopped green onions, crushed peanuts, and fried shallots.

4

Assemble the Dish

  1. Arrange the cooked vermicelli noodles on a serving plate or bowl.
  2. Add minced garlic and stir until fragrant
  3. Place the fried tofu, sliced pork, lettuce, fresh herbs, cucumber, green onions, crushed peanuts, and fried shallots on separate serving plates or bowls.

5

Serve

  1. To enjoy, take a portion of noodles and add desired toppings (tofu, pork, herbs, etc.) onto your plate. Dip the ingredients into the mam tom sauce and enjoy the burst of flavors.
