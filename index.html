A Culinary Map of Vietnam

Food from Northern Vietnam is filled with delicate yet well balanced meals that are flavorful and easy to enjoy. This can be seen in dishes such as pho and banh cuon.

Central Vietnam is known for its rice flour cakes and various types of noodles. Its hot and humid climate is reflected in its cuisine, as it tends to be spicier compared to other regional dishes.

Influenced by other cultures such as French, Cambodian, Chinese, and Thai cuisine, Southern Vietnam has a wide variety of dishes that implement various spices and tend to have sweeter flavor combinations.
